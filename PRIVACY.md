# decibri Contributor Privacy Notice

When you sign the decibri Contributor License Agreement, Venture Consulting
Group Pty Ltd trading as Decibri ("we") records the following: your GitHub
account ID and username, the version of the agreement you signed, the date and
time, and a reference to the pull request where you signed. We do not collect
your email address, IP address, or device information as part of this record.

We use this to confirm that contributions to decibri are covered by a signed
agreement, and to update pull request checks accordingly. Our lawful basis is
the performance of our agreement with you, and our legitimate interest in
keeping clear provenance for the project's licensing.

Your signature comment on the pull request is the primary, permanent record of
your agreement. The structured signature record is stored in a private
repository on GitHub, which processes this data on our behalf under its Data
Protection Agreement.

## Retention

We keep signature records for as long as your contribution remains part of the
project, so that we can establish and defend the licensing of the code.
Signature records may be retained where a contribution remains in the project
and retention is necessary to support legal claims. If your contribution was
never merged, or has since been removed from the project, we honour an erasure
request in full.

## Access, correction, and removal

You may request access to, correction of, or erasure of your record by emailing
privacy@decibri.com. We respond within one calendar month. Where a record is
retained because your contribution remains in the project, we will tell you what
is retained and why.
